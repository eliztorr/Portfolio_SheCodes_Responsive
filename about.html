Hi! I'm Elizabeth

Born and raised in Barcelona, I studied translation and language interpreting specializing in French, German and English language. I've lived in France, Germany and Italy and I've travelled a bit around the World.

After working for the last 15 years in sales administration I decided I was ready for a change. I love to learn and I was looking for something more technical than my current job. Last year I came across a SheCodes add and decided to try the free coding class. I liked it so much that I enrolled right away. A few months later here I am, building my own portfolio.
I'm also interested in learning Business Analytics and I'm following a few courses and workshops whenever I have a bit of time for it.

Curious by nature, I love History, Greek Mythology but also science, especially understand how the brain works. In my free time I like to read a good mystery book, relax doing some yoga, watch a movie and learn Japanese.
